## 🌍 Our Mission

**Ormus Labs** builds highly secure, declarative, and engine-agnostic ReBAC (Relationship-Based Access Control) tooling. 

We believe that authorization should be decoupled from business logic. We build the "glue" that bridges the gap between your application code, your primary databases, and distributed authorization graphs (like OpenFGA, SpiceDB, and Permify).

---

## 📦 The Ecosystem

### 🐍 [django-rebac](https://github.com/OrmusLabs/django-rebac)
A declarative, strictly-typed Zanzibar integration for Django. 
* Seamlessly sync models via the **Transactional Outbox** pattern.
* Secure DRF views with zero-business-logic mixins.
* Inject high-performance, batch-evaluated permission flags directly into JSON serializers.

### 🌐 [central-auth](#) *(Coming Soon)*
The ultimate DSL manager and centralized ReBAC server.

## 🏛️ Why "Ormus"?

Historically, the Kingdom of Ormus was a powerful maritime empire located on **Ormus Island**, controlling the **Strait of Hormuz** in the Persian Gulf. Because of its geography, it acted as one of the world's most critical strategic chokepoints and gateways for global trade.

We chose this name because an enterprise authorization server serves the exact same purpose. It is the ultimate strategic gateway for your infrastructure—a single, highly fortified chokepoint where every user request is inspected, evaluated, and either securely routed or blocked.
